About KittyVerse 🐾

KittyVerse is a cute and smart shelter management platform dedicated to helping rescue centers care for stray cats and connect them with loving families. We build tools to make every shelter operation simpler, so you can spend less time on paperwork and more time with the kitties.

What KittyVerse Actually Does 🐾

🐾

Rescue Case Records

Log new rescue intakes with detailed information on breed, physical traits, location, and initial condition status.

🐾

Medical Tracking

Keep histories of checkups, treatments, and vaccination milestones up to date so every kitty stays healthy and clean.

🐾

Adoption Pipelines

Organize incoming applications, run background reviews, and manage interview schedules to find correct home matches.

🐾

Fanny AI Assistant

Our cat helper assistant draft adoption profiles, write summaries, and analyze compatibility scores to help you make decisions.
